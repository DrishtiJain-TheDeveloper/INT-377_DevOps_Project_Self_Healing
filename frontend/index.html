Ocean Zones

0 – 200m

Sunlight Zone

Most ocean life lives here. Sunlight penetrates fully, supporting photosynthesis and rich biodiversity.

200 – 1000m

Twilight Zone

Dim, cold, and under pressure. Many creatures here are bioluminescent to communicate and hunt.

1000 – 4000m

Midnight Zone

Completely dark. Creatures rely on chemosynthesis and have adapted to extreme pressure.

4000m+

The Abyss

Near-freezing temperatures, crushing pressure. Only the most extreme life forms survive here.
